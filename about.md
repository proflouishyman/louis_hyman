      <p>Louis Hyman is the author of <em>Temp</em>, <em>Borrow</em>, <em>Debtor Nation</em>, and other collections and articles. He is currently researching a history of e-commerce.</p>
      <p>Originally from Baltimore, he holds a BA in history and mathematics from Columbia University and a PhD in American history from Harvard University. A former Fulbright scholar and McKinsey associate, he is a founding editor of Columbia Studies in the History of U.S. Capitalism and directs the History of Capitalism Summer Camp.</p>
      <p>Louis enjoys speaking with book clubs and organizations via Zoom or Skype.</p>
